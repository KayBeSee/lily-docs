# Receiving Bitcoin

Receiving bitcoin is simple with Lily. After setting up your vault or hardware wallet, simply go to the receive page, select the account you want to receive bitcoin in, and you will be displayed a new address.

<img
  src={ReceivePage}
  alt="Lily Wallet receive page" />

Lily follows bitcoin best practices by always displaying an unused address on this screen.
