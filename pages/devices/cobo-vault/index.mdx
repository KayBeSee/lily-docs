## Setup Instructions

Cobo Vault is an airgapped hardware wallet that uses a camera and QR codes to transfer data.

#### 1) Turn on device and write down 24 word seed

Go through the setup process of setting up your Cobo Vault. There are [detailed instructions](https://support.cobo.com/hc/en-us/articles/360019127794-Setting-up-your-Cobo-Vault) on their website.

#### 2) Open the multisig options menu and click "Show/Export XPUB"

To get to the multisig page on the Cobo Vault:

1. Unlock the device
2. Click the <MenuIcon /> icon in the top left
3. Select "Multisig Wallet"
4. Click the <DotsIconHorizontal /> icon in the top right

<Image alt="Cobo Vault homescreen" src={MultisigMenu} />

#### 3) In Lily, click the <DotsIconVertical /> icon and select "Import from QR Code"

<img alt="Lily Wallet import from QR menu open" src={ImportFromQr} />

#### 4) Scan the QR Code from Cobo into Lily

<Image alt="Scan QR into Lily Wallet" src={ScanToLily} />

#### 5) Success

That's it! Once the camera scans the QR code, the camera should disappear and dispaly the Cobo Vault has imported.

<img alt="Successfully imported Cobo Vault" src={Success} />
